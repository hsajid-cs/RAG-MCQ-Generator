#### **Solution:**

- (i) *fg*(*x*) = *f*(*g*(*x*)) = *f*(*x*^2 − 1) = 2(*x*^2 − 1) + 1 = 2*x*^2 − 1
- (ii) *gf*(*x*) = *g*(*f*(*x*)) = *g*(2*x* + 1) = (2*x* + 1)^2 − 1 = 4*x*^2 + 4*x*
- (iii) *f*^2(*x*) = *f*(*f*(*x*)) = *f*(2*x* + 1) = 2(2*x* + 1) + 1 = 4*x* + 3
- (iv) *g*^2(*x*) = *g*(*g**x*) = *g*(*x*^2 − 1) = (*x*^2 − 1)^2 − 1 = *x*^4 − 2*x*^2

We observe from (i) and (ii) that *fg*(*x*) ≠ *gf*(*x*).
