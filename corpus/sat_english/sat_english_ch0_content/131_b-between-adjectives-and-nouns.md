## B. Between Adjectives and Nouns

This rule is particularly important when you are working with multiple adjectives or lists. Though commas are required between items in a list, no comma should be placed between the final adjective and the noun.

Incorrect: Headquartered in New York City, the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences (NATAS) is a well-known national, organization with local chapters in cities around the United States.

Correct: Headquartered in New York City, the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences (NATAS) is a well-known national organization with local chapters in cities around the United States.
