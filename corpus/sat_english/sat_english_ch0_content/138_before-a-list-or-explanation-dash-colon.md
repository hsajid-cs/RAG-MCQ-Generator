## Before a List or Explanation: Dash = Colon

A dash can also be used in place of a colon, for the purpose of explaining or clarifying. When this is the case, a sentence that makes sense as a complete thought must be placed before the dash.

Correct: Impressionist paintings have several major characteristics - a focus on outdoor scenes, an emphasis on the interplay of light and dark, and a sense of movement.

Correct: The bowhead whale is thought to be the longest-living mammal in the world that is, it can survive for up to 200 years.

The dash vs. colon distinction is purely stylistic. As a result, you will not be asked to choose between an answer with a colon and one with a dash unless one of the options is clearly preferable for other reasons.
