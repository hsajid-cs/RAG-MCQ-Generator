## 28. D: Sentence vs. Fragment

Semicolon $=$ comma + and. If both appear as answers, both must be wrong because no question can have more than one right answer. That eliminates A) and C). B) creates a fragment when plugged into the sentence because it
eliminates the subject, I. D) correctly places a comma between a dependent clause (Using a website that connected freelance workers with individuals and companies seeking help with shortterm projects) and an independent clause (I found an editor as well as a cover artist).
