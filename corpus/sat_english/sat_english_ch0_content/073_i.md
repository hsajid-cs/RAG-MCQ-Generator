## I

A) NO CHANGE
B) market, and remember
C) market. Remember
D) market; remember
B), C), and D) are grammatically equivalent, so all of these answers can be eliminated. When you encounter this pattern, you should of course double-check the remaining option to make sure that it makes sense, but in general, you can assume that it will be right. In this case, A) is correct because it places a comma between a dependent clause and an independent clause.
