## Coordinating Conjunctions

There are seven coordinating conjunctions, collectively known by the acronym FANBOYS.
For, And, Nor, But, Or, Yet, So
The most common FANBOYS conjunctions are and \& but, so they should be your primary focus. We'll talk a lot more about FANBOYS in the next chapter, but for now you should know that a single clause beginning with one of these conjunctions is not a complete sentence.

Fragment: And today, tomatoes are cultivated in greenhouses around the world.
Sentence: Today, tomatoes are cultivated in greenhouses around the world.
