## Whereby

Whereby means "by which" or "according to which." It is typically used to introduce a description of systems, methods, or processes. Although constructions involving this word do tend to sound somewhat awkward, they may appear in correct answers.

Correct: Desalination is a process whereby salt and other minerals are removed from water in order to produce a liquid that is suitable for human consumption.
