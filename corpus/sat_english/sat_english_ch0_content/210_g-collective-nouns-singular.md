## G. Collective Nouns = Singular

Collective nouns are singular nouns that refer to groups of people. Common examples include agency, institution, school, committee, jury, city, country, company, university, and team. Although such nouns are sometimes used with plural verbs in informal writing, the SAT only considers singular verbs correct.

Incorrect: For the past several years, the theater company have traveled to various schools throughout the city in order to expose students to classic works.

Correct: For the past several years, the theater company has traveled to various schools throughout the city in order to expose students to classic works.
